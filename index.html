THE LAST CLAW


The FIRST ORDER reigns. Having decimated the peaceful Republic of Kittens, Supreme Leader Pawcheeks now deploys the merciless legions of cats to seize military control of the cat galaxy. Only General Fiona Meow's band of RESISTANCE cat fighters stand against the rising dog tyranny, certain that Claw Master Bob Couchscratcher will return and restore a spark of hope to the fight. But the RESISTANCE has been exposed. As the FIRST ORDER speeds toward the rebel dog base, the brave cat heroes mount a desperate escape....
